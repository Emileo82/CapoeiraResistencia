MEET RODERICK J. DAVIS

Roderick J. Davis (Balanço) began intensive training under the instruction of Mestre Panão in 2005. Within a year, through hard work and dedication, he brought Mestre Panão to his community to start Capoeira Resistência in Richmond, VA.

For more than a decade, Balanço has studied and trained directly with, taught, and traveled all over the US and Brazil with Mestre Panão. In 2015, he graduated as one of Mestre Panão's first American Instructors.

After 15 years of unwavering dedication to the art and his Mestre, Balanço was promoted to the rank of Contra Mestre in August 2024. He has been teaching the community of Richmond, VA since 2021, bringing a wealth of tradition, agility, and resistance to every class.
